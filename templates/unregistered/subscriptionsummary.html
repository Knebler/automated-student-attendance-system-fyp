Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll only pay the difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for enterprise plans.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
